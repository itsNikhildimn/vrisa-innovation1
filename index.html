Modular Prefab Structure

Modular construction is the process of creating a structure on-site/ off site under controlled conditions, with the same norms and standards as conventionally built buildings, but in about half the time. The approach involves producing buildings in modules that are then assembled on-site.
The simplest way to explain modular constructions is to imagine several modules/parts that are prefabricated at a factory and then transported and placed on-site. Because Vrisa creates personalised/ customised designs, we don't have a fixed module and the entire building is made On-site.
These constructions are made up of steel framing and ready-built walls that are simple to assemble and take little time to complete. The majority of the products used are almost finished, saving a significant amount of time.
